# Just-Cinnamon (Só Canela) 🪵☕
> *"Tech with friends, coffee in hand, and the occasional kick in the shin."*

Welcome to **Just-Cinnamon**. We are a group of friends who believe that the best code is written with laughter, good vibes, and maybe a few "caneladas" (brute-force solutions) along the way. We translated "Só Canela" to "Just-Cinnamon" to sound fancy, but we know the truth.

---

### 🦵 The "Canelada" Philosophy
In Brazilian football, a "canelada" is a clumsy kick. In our code, it's that one-liner hack that shouldn't work, but somehow runs the whole production environment. 

*   **Friendship First:** We build things together. If the code breaks, we at least have someone to blame (just kidding, we fix it together).
*   **Spice it Up:** Like cinnamon in coffee, we add a little extra flavor to every project we touch.
*   **Zero Ego:** We're "Só Canela"—we don't take ourselves too seriously, but we take our results very seriously.

### 🛠 What’s Brewing?
Our kitchen (repositories) is always full of interesting experiments:
- **Sweet Scripts:** Small tools that make life easier.
- **Experimental Rolls:** New frameworks we are testing until they break.
- **Friendship-Powered APIs:** Robust backends built on trust and Go.

---

### 📜 The Bro-Code (Rules of the House)
1.  **Don't hide the canelada:** If it's a hack, document it.
2.  **More Coffee, More Code:** The official fuel of Just-Cinnamon.
3.  **Leave the camp cleaner:** Always leave the repo better than you found it.

### 👋 Join the Circle
We’re just a bunch of friends building cool stuff. Take a look at our repos, open an issue, or just send a virtual cinnamon roll our way.
